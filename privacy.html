Privacy Policy for MTH Spin Tools

Last Updated: October 2025

MTH (Multi-tool Hub) Spin Tools ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our "Wheel of Fortune" application (the "Service") handles information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Our Service is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our tool. We **do not collect, track, or store** any personal data on our servers, including:

  • Names or text entered into the wheel.
  • IP addresses or location data.
  • Browser or device information.

2. Local Storage (localStorage)

Our Service uses your browser's "localStorage" feature to provide the "Winners History" functionality. This data (winner's name and contact link) is stored **only on your own computer or device** within your web browser. This data is never transmitted to us or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's cache and site data.

3. Third-Party Ads

Our Service may display advertisements from third-party networks like Adsterra. These networks may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites. We do not control the data collection practices of these third parties. We recommend you review their respective privacy policies for more information.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
